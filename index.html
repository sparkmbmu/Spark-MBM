About SPARK

SPARK is the coding club of MBM University, igniting passion for programming and technology. We organize coding competitions, hackathons, workshops, and mentoring sessions to empower students to become real-world coders and innovators.

Club Highlights

Events

Regular coding contests, hackathons, and quizzes to boost your skills.

Workshops

Hands-on workshops on latest tech trends and real-world projects.
